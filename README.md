Ignite Legal Ops — Website

A premium, US-focused legal operations and attorney-supervised paralegal support platform built for law firms.

This repository contains the website structure, content architecture, compliance framework, and implementation blueprint for Ignite Legal Ops — a B2B legal process outsourcing (LPO) platform serving US law firms across estate planning, probate, civil litigation, and legal operations.

🚀 Project Overview

Ignite Legal Ops is designed to:

Position the company as a structured, attorney-supervised legal operations partner

Generate qualified B2B leads from US law firms

Demonstrate compliance, confidentiality, and security depth

Clearly differentiate from generic outsourcing or virtual assistant services

This website is built with a compliance-first architecture and a conversion-focused structure.

🎯 Target Audience

Solo US attorneys

Small to mid-sized law firms

Estate planning practices

Probate and guardianship firms

Litigation teams

Growing firms seeking scalable operational support
